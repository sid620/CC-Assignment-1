     1	FEL Computing will announce Release 3.0 of LANlink-DECnet at Networld
     2	Boston on February 11, 1992. LANlink-DECnet is a DECnet gateway for PC
     3	LANs. New features include Windows 3.0 and Desqview compatibility,
     4	132-column on-screen terminal emulation, NDIS driver support, and expanded
     5	diagnostic tools. LANlink-DECnet allows PC applications to access DEC
     6	hosts through the LAT and Interrupt 14 interfaces. It supports all PC
     7	LANs, including Novell Netware, Banyan VINES, and LAN Manager, as well as
     8	all LAN technologies, including Ethernet and Token Ring.
     9	
    10	FEL Computing will also announce Release 3.0 of Mobius at Networld. Mobius
    11	provides seamless integration of PCs and PC LANs with DEC VAX computers.
    12	Its new features include Windows 3.0 compatibility, 132-column on-screen
    13	terminal emulation, and enhancements for running on fast CPUs, such as 486
    14	class computers. Mobius can operate through the LANlink-DECnet gateway for
    15	use on PC LANs.
    16	
    17	Also at Networld, FEL Computing will introduce Printway, a PC LAN/VAX print
    18	gateway. Printway allows VAX/VMS terminal users to print to PC LAN
    19	printers. It also allows PC LAN workstations to send printer output to
    20	VAX/VMS printers. All PC LAN technologies are supported, including
    21	Ethernet and Token Ring.
    22	
    23	Lastly, FEL Computing will announce TransPOST/VAX. TransPOST/VAX allows
    24	VINES and VAX users to exchange mail messages transparently. It supports
    25	certification of mail messages, a delayed mail delivery feature, and the
    26	ability to attach ASCII files to messages.
