    88	regeneration as ADI's 8-port model, the AQ-HUB-8E. Unused ports do not
    89	require termination, and there's a visible diagnostic LED for viewing
    90	network activity.

    98	ù It is powered from either a server or workstation
    99	ù It has a diagnostic LED for monitoring network activity
