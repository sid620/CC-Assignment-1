     1	BOSTON, Mass. -- (February 11,1992) -- Alpha Wire will be demonstrating its
     2	newly introduced network management product line here at NETWORLD in Booth
     3	#3915. Alpha's Network Management products provide users with an
     4	integrated wiring system featuring cable, connectors and tools to support
     5	Ethernet, Arcnet, Token Ring and AppleTalk networks.
     6	
     7	Ethernet products feature unshielded twisted pair (UTP), transceiver and
     8	"thick" and "thin" coaxial cables. Each cable type is supported by a
     9	corresponding connector that provides the user with a method of
    10	terminating or interconnecting each of these cables. Token Ring products
    11	include Type 1, Type 2, Type 3, Type 6 and Type 9, available in plenum and
    12	non-plenum versions. Other products include the IBM type data connector,
    13	D-Subminiature connectors, modular plugs and jack, coax connectors and
    14	wall plates. Arcnet products include UTP, RG-62 and the corresponding
    15	connectors and tooling. Appletalk products include shielded and unshielded
    16	twisted pair cables and the corresponding connectors and tooling.
    17	
    18	The Network Management products meet or exceed industry standards such as
    19	IEEE 802.3, 802.5 and EIA/TIA 568 to facilitate system installation,
    20	reconfiguration and maintenance. All Alpha connectors are completely
    21	compatible with recommended Alpha cables for total system reliability.
    22	
    23	"Alpha has been working together with our core group of over 2,000
    24	distributors to develop the appropriate products and marketing strategies
    25	for these network management products. We are very pleased with the
    26	outcome of this development and look forward to providing end users with
    27	the quality wire products they expect from Alpha," said Grant McLennan,
    28	president of Alpha Wire.
    29	
    30	Alpha also offers a wide range of computer interconnect cables for RS 232
    31	applications that fit most computer systems and process control equipment.
    32	Some cables offer capacitance as low as 12.5 pF/ft.
