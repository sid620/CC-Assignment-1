     1	Santa Monica, CA, January 6, 1992 -- Retix, a leading worldwide supplier of
     2	Open Networking products, today announced that it has begun shipments of
     3	its Microsoft Mail for Macintosh Gateway to X.400. The gateway provides
     4	Microsoft Mail (MS Mail) users with direct access to X.400 network users
     5	worldwide.
     6	
     7	The MS Mail Gateway links users of MS Mail for the Macintosh to users of
     8	other leading LAN-based electronic mail (e-mail) products, mainframe and
     9	mini-computer based e-mail systems and public and private X.400 services.
    10	The gateway works in conjunction with Retix' OpenServer 400 to provide MS
    11	Mail-to-X.400 file conversion and transmission facilities. Using the
    12	standard X.400 data format, messages can be exchanged between otherwise
    13	incompatible e-mail programs, regardless of the computing platform.
    14	
    15	Pricing for the gateway begins at $1,795, U.S. list. For more information,
    16	contact Retix Inside Sales at 1-800-255-2333 or 310-828-3400.
    17	
    18	Retix is a leading worldwide supplier of Open Networking products to the
    19	end user and OEM markets. Founded in 1985, Retix designs, manufactures and
    20	markets both OSI software and internetworking bridges and routers to end
    21	users, OEMs, systems integrators, value-added resellers and distributors.
    22	Retix has more than 200 OSI original equipment manufacturer (OEM)
    23	licensees throughout the world and has sold over 20,000 bridges and
    24	routers. Retix' shares are traded on the NASDAQ National Market System
