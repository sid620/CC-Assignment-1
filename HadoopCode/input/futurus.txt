     1	FUTURUS' RIGHT HAND MAN BECOMES FUTURUS TEAM
     2	
     3	February 10, 1992, Atlanta, Georgia -- Futurus Corporation announced today
     4	that their Right Hand Man product line name has changed to Futurus Team
     5	effective March 1,1992. The Futurus products involved are:
     6	
     7	Futurus Team for DOS Futurus Team for Windows Futurus Team Remote Futurus
     8	Team WAN
     9	
    10	"The Futurus Team name is indicative of the work we've done in the
    11	electronic mail and groupware environments," said Futurus President J.
    12	Robert Martinson. "Futurus Team is a more concise description of our
    13	products and better positions us in the LAN applications software market,"
    14	he added.
    15	
    16	Note that Futurus Lite Mail, an electronic mail package for first-time
    17	network users, has not changed its name.
    18	
    19	Futurus Corporation is a privately-held developer of LAN-based electronic
    20	mail and groupware software packages. The company's flagship product,
    21	Futurus Team for DOS, formerly Right Hand Man, has won numerous editorial
    22	awards including PC Magazine's Editor's Choice and Infoworld's Best in its
    23	Class. Futurus Corporation was founded in 1987 by J. Robert Martinson and
    24	is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.

    28	FUTURUS LITE MAIL NOW INCLUDED IN EAGLE NETWARE LITE ETHERNET STARTER KIT
    29	
    30	ATLANTA, GEORGIA, February 10,1992 - Eagle Technology and Futurus
    31	Corporation announced today that Futurus Lite Mail software will be
    32	included in the Eagle NetWare Lite Ethernet Starter Kit beginning March
    33	1,1992. Futurus Lite Mail is the first electronic mail and groupware
    34	package bundled with Eagle Ethernet adapters and Novell NetWare Lite.
    35	
    36	The Eagle NetWare Lite Ethernet Starter Kit is distributed through Eagle's
    37	established channel of domestic and international distributors. The price
    38	of the Eagle NetWare Lite Ethernet Starter Kit is $615 (U.S.) suggested
    39	list price. The Eagle one node NetWare Lite Ethernet Add-On Kit is $312
    40	(U.S.) suggested list price.
    41	
    42	"By offering our customers a true starter kit, including electronic mail,
    43	Eagle advances its commitment to provide cost effective network
    44	computing," said Ken Lamneck, Managing Director of Eagle. "Because Lite
    45	Mail is perfectly suited to NetWare Lite and based on Futurus' five years
    46	of successful experience, Lite Mail was the obvious choice," he added.
    47	
    48	Futurus Lite Mail is designed to meet the needs of first-time network users
    49	through a comfortable and intuitive interface. In addition to low memory
    50	overhead, the Terminate and Stay Resident (TSR) software features
    51	electronic mail for message sending, replying, forwarding, and archiving.
    52	Message Handling System (MHS) compatibility provides seamless sending and
    53	viewing of faxes as well as gateway communications with other hosts such
    54	as MCI Mail and X.400. Other features include file attachments, carbon
    55	copying, blind carbon copying, and unique "bumpable" technology to easily
    56	and economically increase the number of allowable users one by one.
    57	
    58	"Futurus Lite Mail provides NetWare Lite users with true person-to-person
    59	connectivity," said Futurus President J. Robert Martinson. "We are pleased
    60	to contribute our five years of market-proven experience to Eagle
    61	Technology," he added.
    62	
    63	According to Ladd Timpson, Product Line Manager of Novell, Inc., "We're
    64	excited that Eagle Technology is adding Futurus Lite Mail to its NetWare
    65	Lite Starter Kit and Add-On Kit. Lite Mail enhances the standard E-mail
    66	capabilities by providing a Chat utility and a Phone Message Center -
    67	features that allow NetWare Lite users to immediately experience the
    68	benefits of networking."
    69	
    70	Included in the Eagle NetWare Lite Ethernet Starter Kit is a coupon that
    71	users can send to Futurus for a Lite Mail calculator module at no charge.
    72	Futurus also offers users the option of upgrading their Lite Mail software
    73	to Futurus Team for DOS for $60 (U.S.) per node. Futurus Team for DOS
    74	provides all of the functionality of Lite Mail plus personal, group and
    75	resource scheduling, notepad, cardex, database, DOS file manager, and
    76	network print queue manager.
