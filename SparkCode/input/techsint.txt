     1	NETWORLD, Boston, February 11-- Ziff Communications today announced that it
     2	has acquired TECHS, International, of Anaheim, California, publisher of
     3	the Technical Encyclopedia of Computer Hardware and Software, the only
     4	multivendor database of technical support information for computer
     5	hardware and software.
     6	
     7	Ziff plans a future release of the TECHS product as part of its Computer
     8	Library CD-ROM product line, according to Seth Alpert, vice president and
     9	general manager of the Computer Library Group. "The TECHS acquisition is
    10	strategic for us," said Alpert, "because it enables us to enter the
    11	critical area of technical support with a definitive CD-ROM product in the
    12	near future."
    13	
    14	The current TECHS product is available on CD-ROM and contains technical
    15	information from over 60 hardware and software vendors including Novell,
    16	3Com, Banyan, AST Research, Cabletron, SynOptics, Madge, and Standard
    17	Microsystems Corp. (SMC). It is especially useful for supporting network
    18	products and offers cross-referenced information about error messages,
    19	hardware and software compatibility, and installation and configuration of
    20	hardware. It has received a number of awards including Product of the Year
    21	from LAN Magazine in 1988.
    22	
    23	TECHS is used by end-user organizations, major computer product
    24	manufacturers, distributors, and resellers in the U.S. and over 20
    25	countries throughout the world.
    26	
    27	TECHS founder Jeff Schwartz welcomes the Ziff acquisition, and will
    28	continue on as Computer Library's director of LAN support products. "This
    29	acquisition means we now have the resources available to substantially
    30	improve the scope and quality of the product," says Schwartz. "We remain
    31	committed to offering the most comprehensive and accessible technical
    32	support information available anywhere."
    33	
    34	For product support or ordering information, TECHS customers should
    35	continue to call TECHS International at (714) 777-7700.
    36	
    37	A division of Ziff Communications Company, Computer Library develops,
    38	markets and supports comprehensive, CD-ROM databases of computer
    39	information that help businesses evaluate, buy, use and manage personal
    40	computers and networking products.
