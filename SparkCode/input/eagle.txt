     1	SAN JOSE, CALIFORNIA, February 10, 1992 -- Eagle Technology and Futurus
     2	Corporation announced today that Futurus Lite Mail software will be
     3	included in the Eagle NetWare Lite Ethernet Starter Kit beginning March 1,
     4	1992. Futurus Lite Mail is the first electronic mail and groupware package
     5	bundled with Eagle Ethernet adapters and Novell NetWare Lite.
     6	
     7	The Eagle NetWare Lite Ethernet Starter Kit is distributed through Eagle's
     8	established channel of domestic and international distributors. The price
     9	of the Eagle NetWare Lite Ethernet Starter Kit is $615 (U.S.) suggested
    10	list price. The Eagle one node NetWare Lite Ethernet Add-On Kit is $312
    11	(U.S.) suggested list price.
    12	
    13	"By offering our customers a true starter kit, including electronic mail,
    14	Eagle advances its commitment to provide cost effective network computing,
    15	"said Ken Lamneck, Managing Director of Eagle. "Because Lite Mail is
    16	perfectly suited to NetWare Lite and based on Futurus' five years of
    17	successful experience, Lite Mail was the obvious choice," he added.
    18	
    19	Futurus Lite Mail is designed to meet the needs of first-time network users
    20	through a comfortable and intuitive interface. In addition to low memory
    21	overhead, the Terminate and Stay Resident (TSR) software features
    22	electronic mail for message sending, replying, forwarding, and archiving.
    23	Message Handling System (MHS) compatibility provides seamless sending and
    24	viewing of faxes as well as gateway communications with other hosts such
    25	as MCI Mail and X.400. Other features include file attachments, carbon
    26	copying, and blind carbon copying and, unique "bumpable" technology to
    27	easily and economically increase the number of allowable users one by
    28	one.
    29	
    30	"Futurus Lite Mail provides NetWare Lite users with true person-to-person
    31	connectivity," said Futurus President J. Robert Martinson. "We are pleased
    32	to contribute our five years of market-proven experience to Eagle
    33	Technology," he added.
    34	
    35	According to Ladd Timpson, Product Line Manager of Novell, Inc., "We're
    36	excited that Eagle Technology is adding Futurus Lite Mail to its NetWare
    37	Lite Starter Kit and Add-On Kit. Lite Mail enhances the standard E-mail
    38	capabilities by providing a Chat utility and a Phone Message Center -
    39	features that allow NetWare Lite users to immediately experience the
    40	benefits of networking. "
    41	
    42	Included in the Eagle NetWare Lite Ethernet Starter Kit is a coupon that
    43	users can send to Futurus for a Lite Mail calculator module at no charge.
    44	Futurus also offers users the option of upgrading their Lite Mail software
    45	to Futurus Team for DOS for $60 (U.S.) per node. Futurus Team for DOS
    46	provides all of the functionality of Lite Mail plus personal, group and
    47	resource scheduling, notepad, cardex, database, DOS file manager, and
    48	network print queue manager.
    49

    53	Futurus Corporation is a privately-held developer of LAN-based electronic
    54	mail and groupware software packages. The company's flagship product,
    55	Futurus Team for DOS, formerly Right Hand Man, has won numerous editorial
    56	awards including PC Magazine Editor's Choice and Infoworld's Best in its
    57	Class. Futurus Corporation was founded in 1987 by J. Robert Martinson and
    58	is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
    59	
    60	Eagle Technology is a leading manufacturer of network interface cards and
    61	related connectivity products providing multi-platform connectivity for
    62	workgroups, departments, and corporations worldwide. Eagle was formed in
    63	1989 and is headquartered in San Jose, California. Eagle's 1991 shipments
    64	place it among the top three network interface card vendors worldwide.
