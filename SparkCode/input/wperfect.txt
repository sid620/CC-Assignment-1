     1	OREM, Utah, January 31, 1992--WordPerfect Corporation today announced the
     2	shipping of WordPerfect Office 3.1 for PC LANs. WordPerfect Office is an
     3	electronic mail and scheduling package combined with other office
     4	automation programs. Users will be able to upgrade their whole domain to
     5	version 3.1 for $199.US/$239.CN.
     6	
     7	WordPerfect Office's E-mail includes folders, status checking, message
     8	retraction, and allows up to 100 attachments. The 3.1 release also
     9	includes a launching feature for the transfer of documents or graphics
    10	into word processing or graphics editing programs. The scheduler program
    11	schedules events, meetings, and office resources over the system.
    12	
    13	WordPerfect Office for PC LANs and WordPerfect Connections, which allows
    14	for LAN-to-LAN communication, are now bundled together. WordPerfect Office
    15	now includes E-mail, Calendaring/Scheduling, and several desktop utility
    16	programs as well as: Remote Mail, improved Directory Services for LAN
    17	Systems Administration, out-bound FAX support, a platform bridge for
    18	connections to Macintosh, UNIX, VMS, and Data General platforms, and an
    19	improved Asynchronous bridge. The license gives users the right to use the
    20	included Connections software on any on-site PC dedicated to connections
    21	activities including async, gateway, and multi-LAN connections.
    22	
    23	With the additional contents, the suggested retail price of a five user
    24	license PC LAN package will remain the same at $495.US/$595.CN. Additional
    25	license-only packages will also be available in quantities of 1, 20, and
    26	100 for $75.US/$89.CN, $1295.US/$1,555.CN, and $5,995.US/$7,195.CN
    27	respectively. The company said its new license-only prices will
    28	accommodate large account purchasing.
    29	
    30	The step to version 3.1 is an important one according to David Clare,
    31	WPCorp director of development. "WordPerfect Office 3.1 is very important
    32	to WPCorp's crossplatform strategy because it creates a bridge between MS
    33	DOS, Macintosh, UNIX, VMS, and Data General systems for exchange of
    34	electronic mail and scheduling services," said
    35	
    36	Product Marketing Manager Andrew Ehat said that "WordPerfect Office 3.1 is
    37	for use in all organizations that are using or considering PC networks
    38	and/or a variety of multi-user platforms and would like to use global mail
    39	and scheduling across these platforms."
    40	
    41	WordPerfect Office is an electronic mail and scheduling package to enhance
    42	PC LAN communication. The product also contains several desktop utility
    43	programs such as a menuing system, flat-file database, calculator, program
    44	and macro editor, and personal calendar. WordPerfect Office has an
    45	approximate user base of 400,000. For more information, contact WPCorp
    46	Information Services at (801) 225-5000.
