   279	The routing scanner is a very powerful part of the dialer. With it you
   280	are able to scan routings or numbers either manually or automaticly.
   281	In automatic scanning mode you are just listening to the connection.
   282	
   283	If you want to scan routings automaticly at first you have to create a 
   284	script file called ROUTSCAN.SCR with all the function you need to make the
   285	line ready to dial like PLAYTRUNK, WAIT, [...] 
   286	Optimze your break to 100% and enjoy the automatic scanner.
   287	An example:
   288	-------ROUTSCAN.SCR-------
   289	WAIT 2000
   290	PLAYTRUNK 29
   291	WAIT 1000
   292	--------------------------
   293	
   294	A nice feature of Version 0.61 is the scan counter. You can change the
   295	maximum counter with the keys < >. When the max-counter is reached the
   296	script file ROUTSPEC.SCR is called. Got it?! You are able to scan routings
   297	for hours without a hangup. You simply bypass the 2 minutes timeout (+49),
   298	that is caused if you do not get a connection. So you would dial a number
   299	that always picks up the line (loops,test number, pbx's or vmb's work fine). 
   300	Then it goes on. You have another 2 minutes now...
   301	An example:
   302	-------ROUTSPEC.SCR-------
   303	WAIT 2000
   304	PLAYTRUNK 29
   305	WAIT 1000
   306	DIAL A09999999999C
   307	WAIT 5000
   308	--------------------------
   309	
   310	Another great feature of Version 0.61 is the carrier scanner. You can scan
   311	modem carriers using bb. It is really great!
   312	Create the script file ROUTCARR.SCR that will init the comport,
   313	send atdt to the modem, log 40 seconds, hangup the modem
   314	An example:
   315	-------ROUTCARR.SCR-------
   316	INITCOM
   317	SENDCOM ATDT
   318	LOG 40
   319	SENDCOM +++
   320	WAIT 200
   321	SENDCOM ATH0
   322	DONECOM
   323	--------------------------
   324	
   325	All new parts of the routing scanner are still beta.
   326	I wrote it some days before the ACCESS ALL AREAS hacking/phreaking
   327	conference.  Anyway... happy scanning!

   399	--------------------------------WHAT-IT-IS---------------------------------
   400	
   401	The first UK Computer Security and Hacking Conference, Access All Areas,
   402	is to be held in London later this year.
   403	
   404	It is aimed at hackers, phone phreaks, computer security professionals,
   405	cyberpunks, law enforcement officials, net surfers, programmers, and the
   406	computer underground.
   407	
   408	This will be a Conference for people that are interested in various
   409	aspects of computer culture.  Respected industry professionals, legal
   410	experts and computer hackers will be presenting talks about such subjects
   411	as illegitimate computer access, telephone fraud, legal aspects and much,
   412	much more.
   413	
   414	It will be a chance for all sides of the computer world to get together,
   415	discuss major issues, learn new tricks, educate others and meet 'The
   416	Enemy'.
